How It Works

A simple, automatic system that asks your customers for reviews at exactly the right moment — without you lifting a finger.

01

We Set Everything Up

We connect to your Google Business Profile and activate your automated review campaign. Setup takes less than an hour and we handle all the technical work.

02

Customers Get a Friendly Ask

After every visit or service call, your customer automatically receives a friendly text or email asking for a quick Google review. One tap takes them straight there.

03

Reviews Roll In on Autopilot

You sit back while reviews accumulate every week. We monitor everything, respond to reviews on your behalf, and send you a monthly report showing your growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a contract or commitment?

No. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with no penalties or fees.

How quickly will I start seeing results?

Most clients receive their first new reviews within the first week of going live. Results grow steadily as more customers move through your campaign.

What do I need to get started?

Just your Google Business Profile and a list of recent customers. We handle everything else — setup takes less than an hour of your time.

Will my customers know I'm using a service?

No. All review requests go out under your business name. Everything is seamlessly branded as you.

What if I get a negative review?

We monitor all reviews and respond professionally on your behalf. Negative reviews handled well actually build trust with potential customers.

What if I don't have a Google Business Profile?

No problem. We'll help you get one set up as part of your onboarding at no extra charge.
